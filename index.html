About Me

I am an assistant professor in the Department of Artificial Intelligence at Yonsei University. I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from POSTECH where I was advised by Prof. Hwanjo Yu . During my Ph.D., I was fortunate to work as a visiting scholar at UT Health, under the supervision of Prof. Xiaoqian Jiang (2014 Summer) After completing my Ph.D., I worked as a postdoctoral research fellow at the UIUC with my advisor, Prof. Jiawei Han.

My research interest lies in applied machine learning, which aims to develop practical ML solutions for real-world applications. I have covered a wide range of data types (e.g., matrix/tensor, text, graph, time series), tasks (e.g., classification, outcome prediction, anomaly detection, retrieval, data generation), and domains (e.g., healthcare, manufacturing, recommender systems).
