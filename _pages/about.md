I am a postdoctoral research fellow in [Computer Science and Engineering](https://cse.postech.ac.kr) at [Pohang University of Science and Technology (POSTECH)](https://postech.ac.kr). Currently, I am working on data mining and machine learning in [Data Intelligence Group](https://di.postech.ac.kr) with my advisor [Prof. Hwanjo Yu](https://sites.google.com/site/postechdm/people/faculty). My research interest lies in effectively learning various high-dimensional structured data based on deep learning approaches. In particular, I have focused on developing practical solutions for real-world applications, which covers a wide range of data types (e.g., matrix/tensor, graph, time series), tasks (e.g., retrieval, classification, outcome prediction, anomaly detection, data generation), and domains (e.g., healthcare, manufacturing, recommender systems).

I received my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Pohang University of Science and Technology (POSTECH) under the supervision of Prof. Hwanjo Yu. I also have co-worked with [Prof. Xiaoqian Jiang](http://xiaoqianjiang.weebly.com/) as a visiting scholar in [the School of Biomedical Informatics](https://sbmi.uth.edu/) at [University of Texas Health Science Center (UTHealth)](https://www.uth.edu/).

Research Interest
------
- Deep learning approaches to real-world applications
- Representation learning on high-dimensional structured data
- Scalable machine learning and data mining algorithms
